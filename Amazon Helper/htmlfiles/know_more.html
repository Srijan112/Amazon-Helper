Amazon Helper is an online product selling website development project held under supervision of Mrs. Ruchi Gupta (Faculty at Department of Computer Science, GLA University).

This project was assigned as a part of Mini Project. The objective of this project is to minimize the difficulties of people by providing them daily needs simply. At Amazon Helper people can simply find their desired item and order it. You are provided both choices of "cash on delivery" as well as "Online payment" which you can choose according to your simplicity. We've created an initial prototype of the website.
